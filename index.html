Jarren's Talking Pet Works

Luna, the Peppy Pika-(chu)

Luna is a special kind of hamster that is connected
to the Pika species. Luna's is unique for her bright
yellow color and thunder bolt shaped tail. She usually
eats a lot for the normal pet, but makes up for it
through her playful, lively personality. The only
phrases she can say is "pika pika" or "pikachu".
People consider her as the adorable pet to get.

Frankie, the Not-So-Angry Bird

Frankie is a red, fluffy creature that is connected
to the "Angry" Bird species. Though, he is hardly ever
angry by any means. Frankie is the type of pet conducts
his actions with a sense of purpose. He is able to kick
back when it's vacation time and be athletic in the gym
when brought there. Frankie talks in a deep human-like
voice unlike his other red angry bird counterparts and
can say almost anything. People consider him as the
very good-listening pet to get.

Shadow, the Daring Raccoon

Shadow is a short raccoon that, moreso, acts like a
cat. He is the type who is a wingman and charismatic
type of pet. Shadow usually likes to venture out
and accomplish many things. Though, he can sometimes
get out of hand if someone corrects or makes fun of
you or him. Shadow speaks in a slightly, screechy
human voice and can also almost any word he knows.
People consider him as the influencing & protective pet
to get.

Ollie, the Supportive Dog-(go)

Ollie is the golden, orange dog that belongs in
the "hot" dog species. He's the more laid back type
of pet. He usually likes to take things calm and
collectedly. Not necessarily lazy, but more relax-
oriented. Ollie is also the type of dog who loves
to travel to many different places. His phrases
usually are simple, in which his favorite line to
say is "This is Fine". People consider Ollie as
the reserved and easily lovable pet to get.

Buddy, the Playful Clownfish

Buddy, well, comes from the clownfish species.
He loves to interact and call people's attentions
a lot of times. Simply put it, Buddy is a people
person. He absolutely loves to talk and crack jokes
with everyone, even with the employees at the pet
shop. Out of all the pets, Buddy has a higher pitched
voice than everyone, but knows the most words.
People consider Buddy as the entertaining and, well,
talkative pet to get.
